# Football_Pass_Network_Map
Repository for building customizable passing networks with MPL Soccer &amp; Pandas. Able to:
1) Extract data of one team
2) Find successful passes of players who started initially
3) Built a pass network map of the team

Analysis:
1) The team prefers to play centrally. Can vary depending on the opponent's team formation
2) The LB & RB like to play high up. On average the team likes to play aggresively
